Wireless Terminology

Wireless phones, also known as cellular phones are handheld phones you can take with you anywhere with service. Our company uses the Advanced Mobile Phone System standard, and is currently deploying our newly developped GSM network. In order to operate with us, you will need a cellular phone which supports either AMPS or GSM (or both) on 850 or 1900 MHz (or both).
