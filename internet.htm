WELCOME TO THE INTERNET!

North-East Telecom is one of the leading internet service providers in the region. Whether you are looking for residential or commercial, North-East Telecom will have you covered without having to compromise a telephone line!

We offer two types of Internet service: dial-up and broadband. Dial-up does not require any special installation apart from a modem, however it will use your telephone line for the entirety of the Internet session. Fear not, as you may be elligible for a second telephone line for a reduced fee. Broadband Internet will include equipment installation, however you will experience much faster connection speeds and will not be using your telephone line in the process! Do note that broadband is only available in selected regions.
